About

I'm a self-taught AI safety researcher from Zimbabwe, building mechanistic interpretability work independently. I started from zero — no formal ML background, no institution, just a Windows 10 laptop with 8GB RAM and PowerShell. I build in public every day because I believe the work matters more than the credentials.

My goal is to do small, rigorous, honestly-reported experiments that I can fully defend — and use them to earn a place in formal AI safety research. Currently targeting interpretability roles and fellowships. Everything I build is open source.

MirrorProbe
Does social pressure leave a fingerprint inside a language model's internal activations — separate from what it says out loud?
Mechanistic interpretability Linear probing Active

Trained linear probes on the residual stream activations of GPT-2 Small and Pythia-160M. Ran four independent control experiments before reporting any result: lexical confound check, cross-topic generalization, output-identical pairs, and cross-architecture replication.
